####  1. Which of the following scenarios is NOT one that lends itself to a cloud-computing solution?


<i class='far fa-square'></i> &nbsp;&nbsp;You work at an animation firm that uses banks of servers equipped with GPUs to render scenes for movies.

<i class='far fa-square'></i> &nbsp;&nbsp;You are training a convolutional neural network with tens of thousands of images to enable it to determine whether scanned documents contain redactions.

<i class='fas fa-check-square' style='color: Dodgerblue;'></i> &nbsp;&nbsp;You want to consolidate unused bandwidth on thousands of personal computers and lease it to customers.

<i class='far fa-square'></i> &nbsp;&nbsp;Your company has terabytes of data that it wishes to archive in at least two geographic locations to minimize the chance of losing the data.

<i class='far fa-square'></i> &nbsp;&nbsp;Your company runs an online retail operation in which demand varies at different times of day and different times of year.

<i class='far fa-square'></i> &nbsp;&nbsp;The marketing department has asked you to write an application that uses AI to monitor Twitter and alert them if tweets targeting the company turn negative.
