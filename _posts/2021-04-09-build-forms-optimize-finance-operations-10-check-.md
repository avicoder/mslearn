####  2. When all requirements for a form pattern have been satisfied, what must be done before you can build and preview the form in a browser window?


<i class='far fa-square'></i> &nbsp;&nbsp;Run the forms pattern report.

<i class='far fa-square'></i> &nbsp;&nbsp;Check the form statistics add-in.

<i class='fas fa-check-square' style='color: Dodgerblue;'></i> &nbsp;&nbsp;Set the form that you want to preview as the startup object for the project.

<i class='far fa-square'></i> &nbsp;&nbsp;Perform a rebuild of the form.
