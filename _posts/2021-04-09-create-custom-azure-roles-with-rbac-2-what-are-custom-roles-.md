####  1. What's included in a custom Azure role definition?


<i class='fas fa-check-square' style='color: Dodgerblue;'></i> &nbsp;&nbsp;Operations allowed for Azure resources and the scope of permissions

<i class='far fa-square'></i> &nbsp;&nbsp;The assignment of the custom role

<i class='far fa-square'></i> &nbsp;&nbsp;Actions and DataActions operations that you can scope to the tenant level
<br />
<br />
<br />

####  2. What commands help you determine what operations to add to a custom role definition?


<i class='fas fa-check-square' style='color: Dodgerblue;'></i> &nbsp;&nbsp;Use 'az provider operation show' to find resource provider operations.

<i class='far fa-square'></i> &nbsp;&nbsp;Use 'List-AzRoleDefinition' to view a built-in role.

<i class='far fa-square'></i> &nbsp;&nbsp;Use 'az role definition list' to view a built-in role.
